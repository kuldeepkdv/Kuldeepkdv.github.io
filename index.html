// About

Engineering the
Future of Manufacturing

I specialize in Body-in-White (BIW) robotic simulation for the automotive industry — from process planning and plant engineering to offline programming and virtual commissioning of welding and assembly lines.

My work spans global OEMs including Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Daimler, Volkswagen, SCANIA, and CEER, where I've optimized cycle times, validated robot reach, and ensured collision-free production cells — all before a single robot hits the shop floor.

Currently based in Gurugram, India, I'm passionate about leveraging digital twin technologies and automation to push the boundaries of what's possible in modern manufacturing.
